Drinks are liquids that can be consumed. In addition to basic
needs, drinks form part of the culture of human society. In a
commercial setting, drinks, other than water, may be termed
beverages.

Lemonade is drink using lemon
juice, water, and sweetener such as
sugar, simple syrup or honey.

Coffee is a brewed drink prepared
from roasted coffee bean, the seeds
of coffee berries.

Tea is an aromatic beverage
commonly prepared by pouring hot
water over cured or fresh leaves.
